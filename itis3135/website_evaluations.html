Website Evaluation

Fresh Prints

This is the official website for Fresh Prints, an apparel company that targets college campuses. In addition to selling all sorts of apparel Fresh Prints also gets students involved through their Campus Manager position. They offer competitve prices and are licensed to operate on most colleges in the UNC system.

  • C - The Fresh Prints website has great consistency! Its very easy to tell the difference between elements on the page. For example the text is bold and contrast nicely with the background and the clickable images are all in rectangles.
  • R - The repition on this page is also very good. All the headers use the same font and color as well as the small paragraphs. As I mentioned previously, the images are also all in the same rectangular format. Overall very professional looking!
  • A - All the headings and text boxes are aligned to the center of the page and the images are also alighned vertically.
  • P - All the information is grouped with a heading and all the images of apparel are grouped together. Great proximity all around!

Yale School of Arts

  • C - Its hard to tell the difference between the text and the background on certain parts of this page. The fact that the page is also moving constantly makes it even worse.
  • R - The only element repition in this page are the green text boxes, otherwise the page uses a lot of random colors and font sizes.
