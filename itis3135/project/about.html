Who Am I

I am Marissa Wallace, a current junior undergraduate student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte working towards degrees in Communications Studies and International Studies. I’m originally from Wake Forest, North Carolina. Currently, I am the Social Media Advisor for a counseling private practice, Transformation Counseling and Consulting, where I maintain and create content for multiple company social media accounts. Outside of this organization, I run a freelance business setting up branding packages and online portfolios for individuals and organizations such as the National Women Veterans of America. I’m proficient in Spanish and Japanese. In my free time, I can be found reading, traveling or watching international TV dramas.
